Old Fashioned

Strong, Sweet, & Timeless

timer 5 mins
local_bar Rocks Glass
bolt Intermediate

Ingredients

Bourbon or Rye Whiskey 60ml / 2oz
Simple Syrup / Sugar Cube 10ml / 1 cube
Angostura Bitters 2 dashes
Water few drops
Orange Peel & Cherry Garnish

Glassware

wine_bar

Rocks Glass

Lowball or Old Fashioned glass

Preparation

1

Muddle the base

Place the sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass. Add two dashes of Angostura bitters and a few drops of water. Muddle until the sugar is nearly dissolved.

2

Add Whiskey & Ice

Fill the glass with large ice cubes (or one large sphere). Pour the whiskey over the ice and muddled base.

3

Stir Gently

Stir the cocktail gently for 20-30 seconds to dilute and chill the drink perfectly without introducing air.

4

Garnish & Serve

Express the oils of an orange peel over the glass, rub the rim, and drop it in. Add a cocktail cherry if desired. Enjoy slowly.
